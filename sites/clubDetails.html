Club Details

This club is for people who want to play the Catan board game with each other. We typically meet every Saturday at 2 pm. We mostly meet in person, but if the situation calls for it, we meet online using CATAN on Board Game Arena. There are certain event that might cause the meeting to be moved to another day to accomodate another event, which is usually a holiday. Tournaments are held on the last two Saturdays (or the date moved to)
